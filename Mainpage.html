Health Tips

Good nutrition and food safety are especially important for older

Eat a well-balanced diet

Include a lot of vegetables, fruits and wholegrains in your diet. They are rich in fibre, protein, and other vital nutrients that are beneficial for your health.

Choose healthier fats

Replace saturated fats with unsaturated fats from olive oil, avocado oil, fatty fish, nuts and seeds to reduce your risk of heart disease and early death.

Hydrate

Drinking enough water will help you lose weight, boost digestion, improve mood and more.

Practice mindfulness:

Mindfulness has been linked to a number of health benefits. It can help anxiety levels,increase grey matter in the brain, and manage chronic pain.
